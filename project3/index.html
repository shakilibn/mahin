No one said that you have to pick between style and durability, and that’s exactly what the Montana Bison Belt by Hanks brings to the table. It features a double-layered construction, made out of two plies of luxurious bison leather, this gives it the thickness that it needs to help it against warping when you attach something like a firearm or a phone. This type of leather is also highly resistant against splitting, cracking or peeling. Out of the box, it comes with a stainless steel roller buckle, held together with Chicago screws, so you can easily swap it whenever you want. Finally, double-needle stitching adds an extra layer of durability to give it that “buy it for life” quality.
